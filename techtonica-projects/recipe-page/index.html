Postpartum Spiced Oatmeal

Help boost your milk supply and maintain your energy levels with this delicious and nutritious postpartum spiced oatmeal. This comforting bowl of goodness is packed with wholesome ingredients that are perfect for new parents. Oats are known to support lactation, providing a steady release of energy to keep you going through those busy days and sleepless nights.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of steel cut oats
  • 2 cups of oat milk
  • 2 tbs of coconut oil
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1/2 cup of pitted, chopped dates
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Pinch of ground cardomom
  • Pinch of all spice
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Directions

  1. Gather and measure ingredients.
  2. Heat 1 Tbs of coconut oil in medium-sized pan.
  3. Add oats to and ground spices to pan and stir continuously for 2 minutes or until toasted.
  4. Bring 2 cups of water and 2 cups of oat milek to a boil in pot.
  5. Add toasted oats to pot and stir.
  6. Lower heat and let simmer uncovered with cinnamon stick, stirring occasionally.
  7. Once thickened add additional tbs of cocunut oil and 1/2 cup of chiopped dates.
  8. If oatmeal becomes to thick add more oat milk to reach desired consistency.
  9. Serve plain or with additional toppings of season fruit, nuts, and/or granola.
